### üéØ Overall Assessment

Cambodia‚Äôs development hinges on sustaining export-led manufacturing while upgrading skills, infrastructure, and governance. Managing climate risk around the Mekong‚ÄìTonle Sap system and improving rule of law will be critical to unlocking higher-value investment and raising living standards.

### üîç Key Insights

1. **Tonle Sap Dynamics**: Fisheries and agriculture rely on the flood pulse; upstream hydrology changes have system-wide effects.
2. **Supply Chain Shift**: Beneficiary of China+1 and ASEAN integration, especially in light manufacturing and bikes/electronics assembly.
3. **Institutional Upgrading**: Investment in education, logistics, and governance reforms are the catalysts for moving up the value chain.
4. **Tourism Rebuild**: Siem Reap‚ÄìAngkor recovery and diversification beyond heritage sites to eco/cultural tourism improve resilience.
